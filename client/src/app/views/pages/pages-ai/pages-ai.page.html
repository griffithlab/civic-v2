AI Integrations

Use tools and scripts to integrate CIViC into your AI workflows and analysis.

The CIViC project defines policies for the use of AI that ensure human accountability by restricting AI's role in core curation and editorial activities. These policies prohibit AI authorship, and require transparency for the use of AI in research or other assistive purposes. Please review the AI Editorial and Curation Policies in the CIViC documentation for more information.
